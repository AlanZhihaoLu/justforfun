What is this?

It translates text to morse code. Type what you want translated into the text field, and then click/tap the "translate it!" button. You also have the option to show the translated signals visually by enabling the dit guide in the "options" menu.

If this is your first time visiting this website, a "dit" is considered 150ms by default. You can change this default in the "calibrate" menu.
